Internet Technology Types

Internet technology varies from one region to another and one service provider to another. Living in rural areas might leave you no other option than satellite internet. While in urban areas, internet connection is available in different forms including:

DSL

  • Cost-effective and Comparatively Cheaper than other Connections
  • Work on Standard Phone Lines
  • Speed Dependent on Proximity to DSL Distribution Point

5G

  • Faster Speed as much as 20Gbps
  • Lower Latency than 4G
  • Limited Coverage and Available in Specific Areas

Cable Internet

  • Faster Upload and Download Speeds
  • Number of Options Available
  • Hidden Fees and Surge in Prices after a Year or Two

Satellite Internet

  • Satellite Internet is Accessible Worldwide
  • Cost-Effective Compared to Mobile Hotspots
  • Hidden Fees and Surge in Prices after a Year or Two

Fixed Wireless

  • No Need for Cables or Wires
  • Low Installation Cost Compared to Installing Physical Lines
  • Transmission Distance is Limited

Fiber Internet

  • Faster Upload and Download Speeds
  • Minimal Maintenance Required
  • Available in Limited Areas
